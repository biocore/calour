calour.io.read_amplicon¶

calour.io.read_amplicon(data_file, sample_metadata_file=None, *, filter_reads, normalize, **kwargs)[source]¶

Load an amplicon experiment.

Fix taxonomy, normalize reads, and filter low abundance samples. This wraps read(). Also convert feature metadata index (sequences) to upper case

Parameters:
  • sample_metadata_file (None or str (optional)) – None (default) to just use samplenames (no additional metadata).
  • filter_reads (int or None) – int (default) to remove all samples with less than filter_reads. None to not filter
  • normalize (int or None) – normalize each sample to the specified reads. None to not normalize
Returns:

after removing low read sampls and normalizing
Return type:

AmpliconExperiment
